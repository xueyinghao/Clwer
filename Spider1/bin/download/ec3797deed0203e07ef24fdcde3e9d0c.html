课程负责人

　　王幼平，博士，教授，硕士生导师。1986年毕业于西安医科大学获医学学士学位；1992年于新疆医科大学获医学硕士学位；2001年3月毕业于日本香川医科大学获Ph.D.学位。博士毕业后分别就职于日本香川医科大学和美国Michigan State University，2001年4月-2002年12月就职于日本香川医科大学担任Assistant Professor (日本文部省教官)；2003年1月赴美国以Research Associate身份就职于美国Michigan State University医学系。在日本和美国工作期间，主要从事教学和医学研究工作，曾担任生理学和病理生理教学工作。

　　2010年10月回国在河南中医学院担任教授。现为美国心脏病学会及美国生理学会会员。目前，主持有国家自然科学基金及省部级科研项目。2006年获得美国心脏病学会（AmericanHeart Association）颁布的Merck奖，该奖用于奖励在高血压研究领域中成绩优异的中青年学者。2012年和2013年分别荣获河南省科技创新杰出人才和河南省教育厅学术技术带头人称号。迄今为止，共发表SCI论文25篇，其中21篇SCI论文为第一作者，影响因子约为130分，总引用次数超过200次。

Youping Wang, Ph.D., professor of medicine, mentor of graduate students. He got his bachelor of medicine after his graduation from Xian Medical University in 1986, and master of medicine after his graduation from Xinjiang Medical University in 1992. In March 2001, he got his Ph.D. after his graduation from Kagawa Medical University in Japan. He began working as an assistant professor (the staff of Japanese Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology) in Kagawa Medical University after getting Ph.D. in the year of 2001. And in January 2003, he left for the US and worked as a research associate in the Department of Medicine, Michigan State University. His job was focused on teaching and research work in Japan and the US. In October 2010, he returned to China, and works as a professor of medicine in Henan University of Traditional Chinese Medicine. He is eligible for membership of American Heart Association (AHA) and American Physiological Society. At present, he is working as a principal investigator on National Nature Science Foundation of China and provincial level research project. In 2006, he gained the Merck award for excellent research on hypertension from AHA. He was awarded the 2012 Henan Provincial Science and Technology Innovative Talents and the 2013 Academic Technology Leaders in the Education Department of Henan Province. So far he has published 25 SCI papers. Among them, 21 SCI papers have been published as the first author. The total impact factor is about 130, and the citation is over 200.
